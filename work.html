[ THE SHORT VERSION ]

Four questions, four answers.

When do I get paid?

Your balance builds with every photo-verified placement and shows live in the app. Add your payment details (Venmo, Zelle, PayPal, Cash App, or check) and payouts are sent against your verified balance.

Do I have to buy or print anything?

No. Printing is covered by the campaign. When you apply, you choose a print shop pickup or shipping, and the batch is waiting under your name.

What counts as a completed placement?

Three taps at each stop: scan the flyer's QR code, select what you placed it on, take a proof photo. That is a verified placement, and verified placements are what pay.

Where do the flyers go?

Legal spots only: community boards, businesses that said yes, permitted kiosks. The app records the structure type of every placement, which protects you and keeps the whole operation clean.
